  Latest Updates

NMMS scheme is now open for only the students of Rajasthan. Students can register till 10/03/2022 , Institutes can verify till 15/03/2022, defect applications can be submitted till 15/03/2022. District nodal officers can verify till 15/03/2022

For Prematric Labour Scheme and Pre Matric Minority Scheme(Central Sector) Institute verification dates have been extended till 15-01-2022 and District/State verification dates have been extended till 31-01-2022

Renewal Applications where there is a mismatch in aadhaar details can submit their applications now

  From AY 2021-22 all grievances related to the different schemes should be addressed to the grievance officer of the scheme who are appointed by the concerned ministries. The details of grievance officers is available under “Services” in the first page of the portal.

  Attention Institutes

The institutes, not having login credentials are advised to fill the KYC Registration Form and get it approved by District Nodal Officer (DNO) or State Nodal Officer (SNO).

  Option to apply for Institute KYC Registration is available under SERVICES section on the portal.

  The detailed operational steps involved in filling the Institute KYC Registration Form is available under "Important Documents" section on the portal.

  Standard Operating Procedures for NSP

  Students applying from the State of Jharkhand in “CENTRAL SECTOR SCHEME OF SCHOLARSHIPS FOR COLLEGE AND UNIVERSITY STUDENTS” of MHRD (DOHE) are requested to enter their 12th Roll code along with their roll no in the application form.

example – Roll Code is 11066 and Roll No is 20168
